Pulao Recipe

Here’s a simple 5-step Pulao recipe:

  1. Prep the Rice
    • Rinse 1.5 cups basmati rice until the water runs clear.
    • Soak for 20–30 minutes, then drain.

  2. Sauté Whole Spices
    • Heat 2–3 tbsp ghee or oil in a pot.
    • Add: 1 bay leaf, 4–5 cloves, 1 small cinnamon stick, 2–3 cardamom pods, 1 tsp cumin.
    • Let them splutter.

  3. Cook the Vegetables
    • Add 1 sliced onion and sauté until light golden.
    • Add mixed veggies (1 cup peas, carrots, beans, potatoes).
    • Sauté for 2–3 minutes.
    • Add 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste and cook briefly.

  4. Add Rice & Water
    • Add the soaked rice and gently stir.
    • Season with salt.
    • Pour in 2.5–3 cups water (depending on rice type).
    • Optional: add a few mint/coriander leaves.

  5. Cook & Rest
    • Bring to a boil, then cover and cook on low for 12–15 minutes.
    • Turn off heat and let it rest (covered) for 5 minutes.
    • Fluff with a fork.
Enjoy your tasty Pulao!
